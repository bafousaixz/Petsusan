Why Should You Use SusanPet?



Everything on one site!

SusanPet is a site dedicated to helping you find your new best friend, and helping pets find new homes. Search with our easy to use pet searcher to find pets in need of a new home near you, or place an advertisement to help your pet find a new home.

Ethically Focused!

We follow the RSPCA’s Guidelines for the Online Advertising of Pets, helping pets find new homes in an ethical and responsible way. Certified listers are assessed on a case by case basis to ensure they are providing adequate care to their animals.

The Choice is Yours

Everyone has a right to decide which home their cherished family members go to. With SusanPet, you can chat with interested pet lovers and have the final say on who your pet’s next guardian is.
